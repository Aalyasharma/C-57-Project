Hi!

My favorite hobby is reading books because it gives you knowledge about many facts. About history, geography, plants, animals, and much more. It widens your imagination and gives you a new perspective about things. It improves your grammar and vocabulary. It makes you thoughtful and knowledgeable. It opens you to many issues around the world and even improves your memory. There have been several books written by amazing authors that I have had trouble putting down. "The Hunger Games" trilogy, the "Ascendance" series, "The Traitor's Game" trilogy, "The Maze Runner" trilogy and it's prequels, the "Percy Jackson" series, and of course the "Harry Potter" franchise. In every one of these extraordinary books, there have been several inspirational quotes, songs, and naturally, the breathtaking characters- Peeta Mellark, Gale Hawthorne, Katniss Everdeen, Hermione Granger, Harry Potter, Albus Dumbledore, Annabeth Chase, Jaron, Darius, Mott, Thomas, Teresa Agnes, Kestra Dallisor, Cinna, and many, many more.
